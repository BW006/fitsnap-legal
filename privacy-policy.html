Privacy Policy

FitSnap - Virtual Try-On & Visual Search App

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how FitSnap ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, display name (when you create an account)
  • Authentication Data: Apple ID or Google account information (for sign-in)
  • Profile Information: Avatar images you choose to save

2.2 Usage Data

  • Search History: Images you upload for visual search, search results, and clothing preferences
  • Try-On Data: Photos you upload for virtual try-on, generated results
  • App Usage: Features used, time spent, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version

2.3 Technical Data

  • Logs: Error reports, performance data, and crash information
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our service

3. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Information Used Legal Basis
Provide Services Images, search queries, account data Contract performance
Process Payments Subscription information, payment data Contract performance
Improve Service Usage analytics, error reports Legitimate interest

Security Authentication data, device information Legitimate interest

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, data storage, analytics
  • RapidAPI: Virtual try-on processing
  • SerpAPI: Visual search functionality
  • Apple/Google: Sign-in services

4.2 Data Processing

These third-party services process your data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies for more information.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Locations

  • Firebase: United States (with appropriate safeguards)
  • API Services: Various global locations
  • Local Storage: Your device (for offline access)

5.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

5.3 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Until account deletion
  • Search History: Until account deletion
  • Try-On Images: Temporarily stored for processing, then deleted
  • Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 2 years

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

For users under 18, we recommend parental consent and supervision when using our service.

7. Your Rights

7.1 Access and Control

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format

7.2 Opt-Out Options

  • Disable analytics tracking in app settings
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Contact us to exercise your rights
